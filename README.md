<div class="container">
    <h1>Empathy in AI: Improving Mental Health Chatbots for Crisis Support and Emotional Well-being</h1>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>System Overview</h2>
        <p>The proposed system, <strong>Empathy in AI</strong>, enhances AI-driven mental health support by integrating professional therapists into chatbot interactions. The goal is to provide empathetic, context-aware responses and crisis-sensitive interventions to individuals experiencing emotional distress.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Key Features</h2>
        <ul>
            <li>🔒 <strong>User Authentication</strong> – Secure login and sign-up for personalized access while ensuring data privacy.</li>
            <li>🗣️ <strong>Anonymous Community Posts</strong> – A forum where users can share experiences and seek advice while remaining anonymous, reducing mental health stigma.</li>
            <li>🤖 <strong>AI Chatbot Assistance</strong> – A conversational agent providing real-time guidance, coping strategies, and mental health recommendations.</li>
            <li>⚠️ <strong>"Need Assistance" Button</strong> – An emergency feature granting instant access to self-help resources and chatbot support when users are in distress.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Therapist Integration in Chatbot</h2>
        <ul>
            <li>💡 <strong>Therapist Matching</strong> – AI-driven recommendations that suggest the most suitable therapists based on user needs.</li>
            <li>📖 <strong>Therapist Directory</strong> – A directory of licensed mental health professionals, enabling direct requests for professional support.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Relevance to Sustainable Development Goals</h2>
        <p>This system aligns with the United Nations <strong>Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)</strong> by addressing mental health challenges and improving healthcare accessibility:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>🌍 <strong>SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-being)</strong> – AI-driven mental health solutions provide affordable, accessible, and scalable support.</li>
            <li>📊 Research indicates that chatbots can help reduce symptoms of depression and anxiety, making mental health care more available globally.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Technology Stack</h2>

            <li>🔹 <strong>Backend</strong> – Python (Flask/Django), Node.js</li>
            <li>🔹 <strong>Frontend</strong> – React.js, Vue.js</li>
            <li>🔹 <strong>Database</strong> – Firebase, PostgreSQL, MongoDB</li>
            <li>🔹 <strong>Cloud & Hosting</strong> – AWS, Azure, Google Cloud</li>
        </ul>

                </ul>
            </li>
        </ol>
    </div>

            <li>Explore the anonymous community forum to share experiences and seek advice.</li>
